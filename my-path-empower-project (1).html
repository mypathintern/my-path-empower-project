Our Mission

Building a community where every person thrives

My Path: The Empower Project believes that every individual — regardless of ability, background, or circumstance — deserves the opportunity to lead a fulfilling life and meaningful career.

Based in Minnesota, we connect people with the resources, mentorship, and skills they need to chart their own course forward.

Programs & Services

Pathways Designed for You

Comprehensive support tailored to every stage of your journey — from exploration to employment and beyond.

🎯

Career Coaching

One-on-one career guidance including resume building, interview prep, job placement, and ongoing workplace mentorship. We partner with 45+ Minnesota employers committed to inclusive hiring.

Learn more →
🧭

Life Skills Development

Practical workshops on financial literacy, independent living, communication, self-advocacy, and daily living skills. Personalized plans meet you exactly where you are.

Learn more →
🏘️

Community Workshops

Free events and classes on topics from digital literacy to wellness, creative arts to civic engagement. Open to everyone, designed for connection and growth.

Learn more →
📢

Advocacy & Resources

Navigating systems shouldn't be a barrier. We help with benefits counseling, accessibility resources, rights education, and connecting you to the support network you need.
